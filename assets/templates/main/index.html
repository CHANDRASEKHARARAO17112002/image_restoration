What is Image-restoration

Image noise is an unwanted distortion or random variation in brightness or color information that degrades the quality of an image. It can arise due to several factors, such as low lighting conditions, sensor limitations, or transmission errors. Noise can obscure important details, making image analysis and interpretation challenging.

Noise removal is an essential step in image processing that enhances visual quality and usability. While traditional filters like median and Gaussian filters remain useful, modern deep learning approaches, including CNNs and GANs, provide superior results, especially in complex environments. By choosing the right denoising method based on the type of noise, significant improvements in image quality can be achieved.
